About the Band

For over 25 years, Sinister Dexter has been bringing the unique sound of great funk horn bands to the San Francisco Bay Area. We're an 11-piece soul machine that keeps audiences dancing all night long.

Featuring our sensational vocalist Rebecca Lipon, a hot six-piece horn section, and a tight rhythm section, we put on a high-energy show that's been described as "Tower of Power, Ozomatli, and Joss Stone in a blender."

Our sound combines the best of classic funk, soul, and R&B with a modern energy that gets every generation on the dance floor. From weddings to festivals, clubs to private events, we bring the right to be funky wherever we go.
